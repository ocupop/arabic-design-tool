Community Tools

People from within communities experiencing challenges can acutely understand them from the inside, but many don’t have access to the same skills, resources, and networks to translate them into solutions.

Working in Jordan with Syrian refugees and vulnerable community members, the Mahali Lab at International Rescue Committee is a community-driven innovation initiative designed to work with community members to identify problems and develop local solutions to them. Mahali has developed a number of tools using a human-centered design approach.

These tools can help you work with vulnerable communities and empower individuals or community groups to frame and communicate the pressing problems they’re facing and enable creative problem-solving.
